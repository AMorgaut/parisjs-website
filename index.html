About

Paris.js is a usergroup focused on Javascript and related topics.

An entreprise host us and sometimes provides beer & pizza! If you want to be a sponsor paris.js, you can send an email on the mailing list.

You can read the Manifesto.

When?

Every last wednesday of the month.
